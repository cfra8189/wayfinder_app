Understanding Music Analytics

Real-Time Analytics vs. Royalty Payments

While you can see "live" stats on who is listening, actual royalty money follows a slower timeline.

Stream Counts

Live / Daily updates

Use Spotify for Artists or Apple Music for Artists

Estimated Earnings

Monthly updates

Check your distributor dashboard (DistroKid, TuneCore, etc.)

Radio/Live Plays

Quarterly updates

Track via ASCAP or BMI

Digital Radio

Monthly updates

SoundExchange for SiriusXM, Pandora

Recommended Tracking Platforms

  • Spotify for Artists - Real-time listener counts, demographics, playlist tracking
  • Apple Music for Artists - Real-time trends, regional performance
  • Soundcharts - Pro tool monitoring 200M+ data points daily
  • Songstats - Aggregates 20+ sources into one dashboard
  • Chartmetric - Historical data and career tracking

Pro Tip: Audit Your Royalties

Compare your tracking data against monthly royalty reports from your distributor to ensure you're being paid for every stream detected. If numbers don't match, contact your distributor.
